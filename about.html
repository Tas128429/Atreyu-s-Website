Parts

The cpu is a small device that processes all of the information coming from the computer so it can output it. Depending on the type of cpu you get depends on how fast or slow it is.

The motherboard is what holds in the cpu and processes the power to make sure the information goes to the correct parts of the rest of the computer.

The GPU

The GPU is a card that is mainly responsible for heavy duty tasks like shaders and very intense exporting or even gaming. The gpu is most reconized as the "Best Part" of a computer which is because of its use in gaming.
