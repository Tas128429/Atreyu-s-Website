Costs

  • Computer costs

    Computers are really powerful and the price definitly reflects that. The better parts in a computer, the more expensive. That is why there are certain computers for certain hobbies. If your a office worker or a work from home type of person a office pc might be what your looking for, but if your more of a gamer, a gaming pc might be your cup of tea.

    Pricing: $400-$4000+

  • Pre Builts

    Prebuilt computers are computers than are build before shipped to you. These are more cheaper than regularly building computers. The parts are compatible and depending on the type of computer depends on the type of computer processing it can handle. Make sure to find the right kind of parts for what your looking to use a computer for.

    Pricing: Can range from $400 to $2000

  • Building your own

    Building your own computer is also an option to experience people that are looking for high end computers. They parts used may be more expensive than buying a prebuild computer. Just remember if your deciding to build your own, make sure your parts are compatible with each other!

    Pricing: Can range from $700 to $3000+
